About

My name is Andrea. I am a Program Manager at Tesla, where I oversee the entire direct materials supply chain for Supercharging products.

I have master's degrees in Mechanical Engineering from the Polytechnic Institute of Turin (2019) and in Systems Engineering (Supply Chain Management) from MIT (2023). Before MIT, I worked at GE Aerospace, where I graduated from GE’s flagship rotational program in manufacturing operations (OMLP) and held roles in production management and process improvement.

I am passionate about the development of new technology. I measure a man’s worth by how positively he impacts his community, and enhancing humanity’s capabilities with technology maximizes such impact.

Outside work, I enjoy learning, practicing and teaching Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. I’ve formed some of my closest friendships on the mats and have grown to appreciate the character development and community bonding aspects of BJJ. I started clubs at MIT, Tesla, and in the parks of San Francisco.
